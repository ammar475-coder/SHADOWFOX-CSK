About Chennai Super Kings

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) was founded in 2008 and is one of the most consistent franchises in IPL history. Known for strong leadership under MS Dhoni and passionate fans, CSK has won the IPL trophy multiple times (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023). The team plays home matches at M. A. Chidambaram Stadium (Chepauk), Chennai.

  • Founded: 2008
  • IPL Titles: 5 (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023)
  • Home Ground: M. A. Chidambaram Stadium (Chepauk)
